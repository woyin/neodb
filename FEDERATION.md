# Federation

NeoDB implements ActivityPub (server-to-server) federation, with NeoDB-specific
extensions for exchanging catalog, list and review data between instances.

The full federation documentation lives in
[`docs/internals/activitypub.md`](docs/internals/activitypub.md) and is published at
[neodb.net/internals/activitypub](https://neodb.net/internals/activitypub).
